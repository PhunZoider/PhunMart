description=[h1]PhunMart[/h1]
description=
description=[b]Shop Smart. Shop PhunMart.[/b]
description=Transform your vending machines into a turnkey server economy for items, skills, traits, vehicles, and more.
description=
description=With PhunMart, vending machines become fully configurable shops that server admins can use to create economies, progression systems, perks, events, and entirely new gameplay loops.
description=
description=Sell weapons.
description=Sell skills.
description=Sell traits.
description=Sell vehicles.
description=Sell rewards for rare items.
description=Sell whatever your server dreams up.
description=
description=PhunMart transforms simple vending machines into [b]powerful gameplay systems[/b].
description=
description=[h2]Key Features[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*]Sell items, skills, traits, vehicles and more
description=[*]Supports server currencies and item-based payment
description=[*]Deep configuration capability, but also works great out of the box
description=[*]Works in Single Player but designed for multiplayer servers
description=[*]Works with modded items
description=[*]Optional integration with PhunZones
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]For Players[/h2]
description=Explore the world and discover machines offering:
description=[list]
description=[*]Rare gear and supplies
description=[*]Skill boosts and XP
description=[*]Traits and perks
description=[*]Vehicles
description=[*]Server currencies and tokens
description=[*]Fast travel terminals
description=[/list]
description=
description=Each machine can have its own inventory, theme, pricing, and rules.
description=
description=What you find in Louisville might be very different from what you find in Riverside.
description=
description=[h2]For Server Admins[/h2]
description=PhunMart lets server operators build deep gameplay systems without writing new mods.
description=
description=Create systems such as:
description=[list]
description=[*]Custom shops
description=[*]Server-wide economies
description=[*]Skill training terminals
description=[*]Trait trading machines
description=[*]Vehicle dealerships
description=[*]Starter hubs and late-game vendors
description=[*]Seasonal or event shops
description=[/list]
description=
description=Gate purchases behind skills, traits, professions, items, or server progression and design entire economy ecosystems around vending machines.
